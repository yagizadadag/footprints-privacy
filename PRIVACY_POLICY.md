# Privacy Policy – Footprints

**Last updated: December 2025**

Footprints is designed with privacy in mind. We believe your journeys should remain personal and fully under your control.

## Information Collection

Footprints does not collect, store, or share any personal data.
The app does not require account creation or user registration.

All trips, statistics, and preferences are stored locally on your device and are never transmitted to external servers.

## Location Data

Footprints does not access or use your device's real-time location.
Any locations shown in the app are based solely on cities manually added by the user.

## Preloaded Content

The app may include preloaded, static content (such as destination insights) to provide informational features. This content is not personalized and does not involve data collection.

## Tracking

Footprints does not use tracking technologies, analytics services, or advertising identifiers.

## Data Deletion

Users can permanently delete all app data at any time using the in-app reset option.

## Changes to This Policy

If the app's privacy practices change in the future, this policy will be updated accordingly.

## Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, you can contact us at: yagizadadag1@icloud.com
